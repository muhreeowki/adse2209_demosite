ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Muriuki Muchiri, Muh(mo) for short. I was born and raised in Nairobi, Kenya.
From an early age, I have been a very curious individual. I have always been fascinated with learning how things work. When I was younger I used to take apart all my toy, trying to figure out what made them move on their own. Through this curiosity I developed a love for learning.

I attended a robotics bootcamp when I was about 11 years old. There I discovered programming for the first time, and how it works. I was amazed at how software could make these different hardware components work together, and how I could use them to bring my ideas to life. I began to enjoy solving problems and creating solutions using software.

I went on to pick computer science in high school and also participated in two more computing bootcamps. This helped furthered my interest in computing. During this time I built numerous robots individually and together with a team such as a self-navigating robot, and a line-following robot that could make decisions. This developed my leadership abilities as I was the leader of my group and It helped me learn to communicate effectively and to listen to other people's opinions.
